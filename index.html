About Us!

Our journey started a long time ago with our ancestors back in the 1800's. We were one of the first gelato masters in the food truck business. We had a variety of the flavors (and still do!) including vaniglia, cioccolato, nocciola, limone, and fragola. We have expanded our flavors and our food truck business through out all of italy and have gelaterie at these locations! We have expanded Gelato Go to the sunny and hot state of Florida in the United States.
